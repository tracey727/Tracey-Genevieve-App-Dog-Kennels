☕ Breaks — everyone gets theirs, no exceptions

Meal break before 5 hours worked; paid rest breaks per your award. Skipped breaks are a psychosocial WHS hazard — GENEVIEVE™ tracks them and flags unfairness patterns.

🔁 Shift rotation & overtime — fairness enforced

Closes must rotate. All time worked must be paid (Fair Work Act) — GENEVIEVE™ counts every unpaid minute and who keeps getting the shifts nobody wants.
